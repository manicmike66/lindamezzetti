busy busy busy

15/9/2015

This month I decided to hold a stall at my son's primary school fete. In order to give it my best I sewed for 10-14 hours a day for a few weeks making new bags, wallets, backpacks etc. I was quite pleased with the sales for that day so am thinking of doing a few more markets leading up to Christmas.
Earlier in the month I made myself a period costume to wear at an art event with The Half Dozen Group Of Artists here in Brisbane. Had to wear that to the fete as it was great advertising for my costume making skills! Didn't get any costume orders though!
